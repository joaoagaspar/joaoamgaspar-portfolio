About Me

Hi, I’m João Gaspar – a photography enthusiast, traveler, and storyteller. My journey began with a simple love for capturing moments, turning them into timeless memories.

Whether it’s the raw beauty of Iceland’s landscapes, the bustling streets of New York, or the untamed wilderness, I’m passionate about exploring the world through my lens.

While I enjoy street photography the most, I never back down from a great adventure into nature’s wild embrace.

Let’s connect and share stories!

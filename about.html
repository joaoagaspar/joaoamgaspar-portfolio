About Me

Hi, I’m João Gaspar – a photography enthusiast, traveler, and storyteller. My journey began with a simple love for capturing moments, turning them into timeless memories. Whether it's the raw beauty of Iceland’s landscapes or the bustling streets of New York, I’m passionate about exploring the world through my lens.

When I’m not behind the camera, you can find me diving into a good book, planning my next adventure, or sharing my experiences on Instagram. Let’s connect and share stories!
